ABC Health Insurance Policy Document

Policy Number: HIP-2026-001

---

Section 1: Policy Overview

ABC Health Insurance is designed to provide comprehensive health coverage for individuals and families. The policy offers cashless hospitalization benefits, reimbursement facilities, and coverage for pre and post hospitalization expenses.

The minimum entry age for this policy is 18 years, and the maximum entry age is 65 years.

---

Section 2: Sum Insured Options

The following sum insured options are available:

* Rs. 5 Lakhs
* Rs. 10 Lakhs
* Rs. 20 Lakhs
* Rs. 50 Lakhs

Policyholders can upgrade their sum insured during renewal subject to underwriting approval.

---

Section 3: Hospitalization Coverage

The policy covers:

* Inpatient hospitalization expenses.
* ICU charges.
* Doctor consultation fees.
* Nursing charges.
* Room rent expenses.
* Operation theatre expenses.
* Ambulance charges up to Rs. 5,000 per hospitalization.

---

Section 4: Waiting Periods

The policy includes the following waiting periods:

* Initial waiting period of 30 days from the policy commencement date.
* Specific illnesses waiting period of 24 months.
* Pre-existing diseases waiting period of 36 months.
* Maternity benefit waiting period of 24 months.

Accidental hospitalization is covered from day one.

---

Section 5: Maternity Benefits

The policy provides maternity coverage up to Rs. 75,000 for normal delivery and up to Rs. 1,00,000 for cesarean delivery after completion of the waiting period.

Newborn baby coverage is available for up to 90 days from the date of birth.

---

Section 6: Pre and Post Hospitalization

The policy covers:

* Pre-hospitalization expenses for 60 days.
* Post-hospitalization expenses for 180 days.

These expenses include diagnostic tests, prescribed medicines, and doctor's consultations.

---

Section 7: Day Care Procedures

The following day care procedures are covered:

* Cataract surgery.
* Chemotherapy.
* Dialysis.
* Radiotherapy.
* Tonsillectomy.
* Dental surgery arising due to accidents.

---

Section 8: Policy Exclusions

The following are not covered:

* Cosmetic surgery.
* Experimental treatments.
* Self-inflicted injuries.
* Drug and alcohol abuse related treatments.
* Treatments performed outside India.
* Non-prescribed medical expenses.

---

Section 9: Claim Process

For cashless hospitalization:

1. Inform the insurance company at least 48 hours before planned hospitalization.

2. Submit the pre-authorization form.

3. The insurance company will coordinate with the network hospital.

For reimbursement claims:

1. Preserve all original bills.
2. Submit claim documents within 30 days.
3. Claims are generally settled within 15 working days after document verification.

---

Section 10: Network Hospitals

The policy has more than 12,000 network hospitals across India.

Cashless hospitalization is available only at network hospitals.

---

Section 11: Policy Renewal

The policy can be renewed annually.

Grace Period:
30 days.

The policy offers lifelong renewability.

---

Section 12: Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Is maternity covered?

Yes. Maternity benefits are available after completion of a 24-month waiting period.

Q2. What is the waiting period for pre-existing diseases?

The waiting period is 36 months.

Q3. Are ambulance charges covered?

Yes. Ambulance charges are covered up to Rs. 5,000 per hospitalization.

Q4. Is accidental hospitalization covered immediately?

Yes. Accidental hospitalization is covered from day one.

Q5. How many days of post-hospitalization expenses are covered?

Post-hospitalization expenses are covered for up to 180 days.

---

End of Document
