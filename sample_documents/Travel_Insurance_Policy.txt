XYZ Travel Insurance Policy

Policy Number:
TI-2026-101

Coverage:
- Medical emergencies up to Rs. 50,00,000.
- Trip cancellation expenses are covered.
- Loss of checked-in baggage is covered.
- Passport loss assistance is available.

Waiting Period:
- There is no waiting period for accidental medical emergencies.
- Trip cancellation claims can be made immediately.

Hospitalization:
- Emergency hospitalization outside India is covered.
- Ambulance expenses are covered up to Rs. 20,000.

Exclusions:
- Adventure sports are not covered.
- Alcohol or drug related incidents are excluded.
- Travelling against medical advice is not covered.

Claim Process:
- Claims should be reported within 15 days.
- Required documents must be submitted online.
